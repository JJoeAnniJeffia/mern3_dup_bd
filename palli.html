Chicken Pallipalayam

Description

Chicken Pallipalayam is a traditional South Indian dish known for its spicy and tangy flavor. Originating from the Pallipalayam region in Tamil Nadu, this dish features succulent pieces of chicken cooked with a blend of aromatic spices, curry leaves, and a hint of tamarind. It is often enjoyed with steamed rice or dosa.

About

Chicken Pallipalayam is a popular recipe among South Indian cuisine enthusiasts. This dish stands out for its rich flavor profile, which is achieved through the use of freshly ground spices and the slow cooking method. The combination of spices, curry leaves, and tamarind gives Chicken Pallipalayam its distinctive taste, making it a favorite for those who appreciate bold and spicy flavors.

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and cook for a few minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Mix in red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  6. Add tamarind paste and curry leaves. Mix well and cook for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or dosa.
